## Who We Are

SYSCLI is an infrastructure and security engineering practice delivering production-grade systems for companies. We design, harden, automate, and operate the networks, servers, and cloud platforms that businesses run on under defined standards, with measurable reliability.

Client engagements are private by default. We publish tooling, references, and internal libraries as open source where it does not conflict with client confidentiality.

## Standards & Practices

We design and operate to recognized industry frameworks:

- Security controls aligned with **ISO/IEC 27001**, **NIST CSF**, and **CIS Benchmarks**
- Least-privilege access and infrastructure-as-code as defaults
- Documented runbooks, change control, and disaster-recovery planning
- Continuous monitoring, logging, and alerting across managed environments

---

## Engagement Models

- **Project delivery** — scoped builds and migrations with defined outcomes
- **Managed services** — ongoing operation, monitoring, and maintenance under retainer
- **Advisory & architecture** — security reviews, design, and roadmap consulting
- **Incident response** — rapid support for outages and security events
